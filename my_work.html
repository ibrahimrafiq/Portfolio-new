A.I Pacman Game

I developed a sophisticated Pacman game using advanced AI algorithms, including depth-first search (DFS), breadth-first search (BFS), A* search, and reinforcement learning. These algorithms enable the agent to intelligently navigate the maze, avoid ghosts, gather pellets, and maximize its score. By integrating these techniques, the agent anticipates and plans its moves, creating a dynamic and challenging gaming experience that showcases the power of AI in enhancing gameplay strategies and decision-making processes.

Premier League Stats Analysis

I used Python for data analysis to evaluate players' shooting statistics in the Premier League season. By leveraging libraries like pandas, NumPy, and Matplotlib, I processed and visualized the data, uncovering insights into shooting accuracy, goals per match, and shot conversion rates. This analysis provided a detailed understanding of player performance, highlighting top shooters and identifying areas for improvement.
